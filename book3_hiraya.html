My Lady Hiraya

ISBN: 9789815266160
Author: Steven SY
Year Published: 2025
Page Count: 308

Description: Five years ago, when Elise’s wife died in a fire, she vowed that she would stop at nothing to bring her back. Her quest leads her to Hiraya, an immortal monster hunter with secrets deeper than the shadows of the haunted manor she calls home. Hiraya, though hesitant at first, agrees to train Elise in the secrets of Chaos, magic that could alter the strands of time themselves. As their bond deepens, Elise and Hiraya find themselves embroiled in an eons-old conflict among the gods of love, destiny, and the future. The choices they make and the love they discover between themselves will shape this world and all the worlds that could ever be. Inspired by Filipino mythology and folk tales, My Lady Hiraya is a romance fantasy novel that follows Elise’s dogged pursuit of her lost love and the love she finds along the way.
